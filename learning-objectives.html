Learning Objectives

After completing this website, learners will be able to:

  • Explain how people process information through visual and auditory channels.
  • Describe what CTML is and why it matters for learning design.
  • Identify key CTML assumptions and connect them to Cognitive Load Theory.
  • Apply CTML principles to improve educational video scripts, visuals, and pacing.
  • Evaluate an educational video and propose CTML-based improvements.
