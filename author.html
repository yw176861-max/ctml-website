Richard E. Mayer

Richard E. Mayer is widely known for research on how people learn from words and pictures. His work connects cognitive psychology with instructional design, and CTML is one of the most influential frameworks for multimedia learning.

Why Mayer matters for educational video

  • Provides evidence-based principles for combining narration, text, and visuals.
  • Helps reduce overload and increase meaningful learning (understanding + transfer).
  • Offers practical guidelines for video pacing, layout, and signaling.
