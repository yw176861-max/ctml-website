Application in Educational Video

Example: Designing a 6–8 minute video lesson on “How Photosynthesis Works”.

Storyboard plan (CTML-based)

  • Segmenting: split into 4 short scenes (intro, inputs/outputs, process steps, recap).
  • Pre-training: define key terms first (chlorophyll, sunlight, CO₂, glucose).
  • Coherence: remove background music during complex explanations.
  • Signaling: use arrows/highlights when showing the leaf diagram.
  • Modality: explain steps with narration + visuals rather than long on-screen text.
  • Redundancy: avoid reading full sentences that already appear on screen.
  • Contiguity: keep labels close to parts of the diagram; sync narration with animation.

Quick reflection prompts

  • Which principle did you apply most strongly? Why?
  • Where might learners feel overloaded? How will you reduce extraneous load?
  • How will you check understanding (quiz/exit ticket)?
